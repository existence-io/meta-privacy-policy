Privacy Policy for Existence Insights

Last updated: October 28, 2025

Existence Insights operates this application, which connects to Meta’s API to provide analytics and insights for authorized Facebook and Instagram accounts. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

Through the Meta API, we may access the following types of data associated with your authorized Facebook Pages or Instagram Business accounts:

  • Aggregated insights such as reach, impressions, engagement, and audience demographics
  • Account and media identifiers (e.g., Page ID, Media ID)
  • Basic profile information (e.g., Page name, username)

We do not collect, store, or have access to personal user data from your followers or any non-authorized accounts.

2. How We Use the Information

We use the data solely to:

  • Display insights and performance metrics to authorized account administrators
  • Analyze aggregated marketing and content performance
  • Improve analytics dashboards and reporting

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. Data obtained via the Meta API is handled according to Meta’s platform policies and your account permissions.

3. Data Retention and Storage

We retain data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes above. Any cached data is deleted or refreshed regularly to ensure accuracy and privacy.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your information with third parties except:

  • When required by law or regulation
  • To comply with Meta’s platform review and compliance requirements

We do not share personal or aggregated insights with advertisers or external partners.

5. Your Rights and Control

You can revoke this app’s access at any time via:

  • Facebook Business Integrations
  • Instagram App Access

Upon revocation, we will no longer receive new data from your connected accounts.

6. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
