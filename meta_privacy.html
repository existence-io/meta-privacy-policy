  <p>Through the Meta API, we may access the following types of data associated with your authorized Facebook Pages or Instagram Business accounts:</p>

  <ul>

    <li>Aggregated insights such as reach, impressions, engagement, and audience demographics</li>

    <li>Basic profile information (e.g., Page name, username)</li>

  </ul>

  <p>We use the data solely to:</p>

  <ul>

    <li>Display insights and performance metrics to authorized account administrators</li>

    <li>Analyze aggregated marketing and content performance</li>

    <li>Improve analytics dashboards and reporting</li>

  </ul>

  <p>We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. Data obtained via the Meta API is handled according to Meta’s <a href="https://www.facebook.com/policy.php" target="_blank">Data Policy</a>.</p>

  <h2>3. Data Retention and Storage</h2>

  <p>We retain data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes above. Any cached data is deleted or refreshed regularly. All data is stored securely using industry-standard measures.</p>

  <h2>4. Data Sharing and Disclosure</h2>

  <p>We do not share your information with third parties except:</p>

  <ul>

    <li>When required by law or regulation</li>

    <li>To comply with Meta’s platform review and compliance requirements</li>

  </ul>

  <p>We do not share personal or aggregated insights with advertisers or external partners.</p>
